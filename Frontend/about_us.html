About MoodNight

Illuminating the path to emotional self-awareness.

Our Mission

MoodNight was created with a simple belief: You cannot manage what you do not measure.

In our fast-paced world, emotions often go unnoticed until they become overwhelming. Our platform provides a safe, private space to log your daily moods, reflect on your thoughts via journaling, and gain insights into your unique emotional patterns. By combining data visualization with psychological archetypes (our Moon Phase system), we hope to make mental wellness engaging and insightful.

How MoodNight Works

We utilize a secure, encrypted system to process your entries. When you log a mood or write in your journal, our backend analyzes the sentiment securely to provide you with analytics, while ensuring your raw thoughts remain private. The "Moon Phase" personality test aggregates your responses to match you with a psychological archetype that best fits your current state of mind.
